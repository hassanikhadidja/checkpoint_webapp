How long does a typical project take?

A standard website takes 4–6 weeks. Full-stack web apps or mobile projects usually run 8–16 weeks depending on scope. We give a detailed timeline during scoping.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely. Many of our clients are early-stage startups. We offer phased approaches to help you launch lean and iterate based on real user feedback.

What if I don't know what I need yet?

That's fine — we'll figure it out together. A free discovery call with our team is the best first step, no obligations attached.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes. After the 90-day post-launch window, we offer monthly retainer packages covering updates, performance monitoring, and feature additions.
